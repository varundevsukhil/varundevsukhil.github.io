Research

Code made open-source in GitHub after publication. Email me questions regarding source and usage.

F1/10 Racecar



Designed a new version of the F1/10 racecar that is capable of V2V/V2X. This allows me to use a fleet of connected racecars to test real autonomous racing frameworks including cooperative and adversarial team racing.

ROS Autonomous Racing Simulator



Created a modular autonomous racing simulator using Gazebo with seamless namespace delineation that helps simultaneous and independent control of each agent. Currently used to teach F1/10 course at UVA.

Sim2Real



Research tool to easily transition between the ROS F1/10 simulator and the real F1/10 racecar. Helps in testing and validating the performance of autonomous racing algorithms.

Adaptive Lookahead Purepursuit



Optimized the lookahead distance at every waypoint based on the curvature of the race-track and performance of the racecar to achieve the fastest possible speed/lap time.

Racecar State Estimation



Designed a vehicular state estimation filter that can accurately predict the pose and velocity of an opponent racing agent. Extensively used for safe & agile autonomous overtaking.
